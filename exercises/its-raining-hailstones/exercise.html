Here's the run-down:

  1. Start with a whole number x.
  2. While x isn't 1 do the following: a. If x is even, divide it by 2. b. If x is odd, multiply it by 3 and add 1. c. Print x.
  3. Repeat step 2 until x is 1.

Though it appears x will always equal 1 eventually, no one has yet figured out why.
